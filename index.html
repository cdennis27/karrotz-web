Some of our process for hoarding and estate cleaning services include:

  • Covering areas and other protection procedures in the surrounding area to protect adjacent areas from damage and contaminants when needed
  • Separating items to keep and items to dispose
  • Removing items to be disposed
  • Disinfecting and sanitizing the affected areas
  • Deodorizing the area
  • Restoring the area to its original condition
  • Organize and inventory all kept items
  • Temporary repairs where needed before possible renovation / construction work begins.
  • Performing a final walk-through with the client to ensure satisfaction
